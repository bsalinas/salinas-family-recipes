Yield: 3 12” cookies

Monster Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 sticks butter (softened)
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla
  • 3 ½ + 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 bag chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Cream butter, sugars and vanilla. Add eggs and beat until fluffy.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine flour, soda and salt.

  3. Add to creamed mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.

  4. Divide into thirds. Spread each third onto a greased 12” pizza pan.

  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes. (Dough can also be baked in pie pans yielding nine 6” cookies.)
