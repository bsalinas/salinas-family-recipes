Makes 12 cups

Wassail

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts apple juice or cider
  • 1 pint cranberry juice
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp aromatic bitters (optional)
  • 2 sticks cinnamon
  • 1 tsp whole allspice
  • 1 small orange, studded with whole cloves
  • 1 cup rum (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in crock pot.

  2. Cover and cook on High 1 hour and then on low for 4 to 8 hours.

  3. Serve warm from crock pot
