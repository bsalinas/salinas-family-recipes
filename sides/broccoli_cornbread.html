8 servings

Juani’s Broccoli Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1 package Jiffy cornbread mix
  • 3/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup onion (chopped)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted margarine
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 10oz package chopped frozen broccoli (partially thawed and drained)
  • 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees

  2. Combine cornbread, cottage cheese, onion, eggs and melted margarine.

  3. Mix well.

  4. Stir in broccoli & cheese.

  5. Pour into greased 9” baking pan.

  6. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
