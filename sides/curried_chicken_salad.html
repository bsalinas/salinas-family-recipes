Yields 6 cups/12 servings

Curried Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayo or Miracle Whip
  • 1 Tbs. curry powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. white pepper
  • 3 cups diced cooked chicken (~3 lbs or 6 chicken breasts)
  • 1 large apple, diced (1Â½ cups)
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced red onions
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup walnuts

Instructions

  1. Mix mayo and spices together to form a dressing.

  2. Add remaining ingredients.

This is good served on croissants at parties. Leave out the curry powder if your guests are not as adventurous.
