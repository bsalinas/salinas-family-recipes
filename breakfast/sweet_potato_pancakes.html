Sweet Potato Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 3½ tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
  • 1¼ cups mashed cooked sweet potatoes
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1½ cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.

  2. In a separate bowl, mix potatoes, eggs, milk and butter.

  3. Stir dry ingredients into mixture.

  4. Pour pancakes onto hot greased griddle.

Breakfast
