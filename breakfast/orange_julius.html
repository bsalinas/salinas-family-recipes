Makes 6 Cups

Orange Julius

Ingredients

  • 1 6 oz. can orange juice (Minute Maid)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 10 to 12 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in blender; cover and blend until smooth, about 30 seconds. Serve immediately.
