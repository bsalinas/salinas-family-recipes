Serves 6

Honey-Baked Chicken

  • 1 3-lb. fryer, cut up
  • 1/3 cup margarine, melted
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 2 Tbs. mustard
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. curry powder

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Arrange chicken in shallow baking pan, skin-side up.

  2. Combine other ingredients and pour over chicken.

  3. Bake 1Â¼ hour, basting every 15 minutes, until chicken is tender and nicely browned. Good served with rice.
