Serves 2

Turkey Black Bean Chili

Ingredients

  • 8 oz extra lean ground white meat turkey
  • 1 cup canned, no salt-added, black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup low sodium chipotle flavored salsa
  • 2 tsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/2 cup mashed avocado
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (15-16 oz)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan sprayed with cooking spray, cook turkey with chili powder and cumin over medium heat.

  2. When cooked, add beans, tomatoes, and salsa.

  3. Cook, stirring occasionally, for about 3 minutes or until heated through.

  4. Top each serving with 1/4 cup of avocado. Serve immediately.
